What We Offer

Services designed
around you

  • Individual Therapy

    "I just need someone to talk to — someone who won't judge me."

    A private, one-to-one space that belongs entirely to you. No agenda, no pressure — just the freedom to say what you've been carrying.

    Book a session

  • Men's Mental Health

    "I should be able to handle this on my own."

    You don't have to. Many men find it hard to ask for help — we get that. This is a space where you can speak honestly, without judgement, and without being told how you should feel.

    Book a session

  • Neurodivergent Support

    "Your brain works differently. And finally, someone gets it."

    Whether you're diagnosed or self-identifying, neurodivergence can raise so many questions about who you are and how you move through the world. We'll support you to find your own answers, at your own pace.

    Book a session

  • Online Sessions

    "I want help, but leaving the house right now feels impossible."

    Everything you'd get in person, from wherever feels safe for you. Secure video or phone sessions, flexible around your life.

    Book a session

  • Anxiety & Stress

    "My mind won't stop. I'm exhausted from worrying about everything."

    You don't have to live in survival mode. We'll help you understand what's driving your anxiety and find real, lasting relief.

    Book a session

  • Grief & Loss

    "Everyone expects me to be over it by now. I'm not even close."

    Grief has no timetable. We offer a compassionate, unhurried space to sit with your loss and find your way through it.

What to expect

How it works

  1. 01

    Choose your counsellor

    Browse our team and pick the person you feel most drawn to. Not sure who to choose? All of our counsellors will be happy to have a chat and help you decide.

  2. 02

    Free first session

    A no-pressure conversation to talk about what has brought you here. No commitment, no obligation, just a chance to take that first step.

  3. 03

    Your sessions begin

    Regular sessions tailored entirely to you, at a pace and in a format that suits your life, online or in person.

  4. 04

    Ongoing support

    Some people come for a few sessions, others for longer. When you're ready to wind down, we'll do that gradually and thoughtfully — so you finish feeling steady, not abandoned.
